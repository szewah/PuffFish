About


Oral Histories preserve your life's stories through recorded interviews.


Stories that can be left to your family and many generations after.

Approach


Clients and I have an initial converstion about their life.

I spend time looking at their public profile, their publications and researching the period of the time.

We look at their photos albums, memorabilia and other items that hold value to them.

We then spend between another 2-10 sessions recording their stories.

I then clean up the audio for a digital copy and transcribe the recordings into a hard copy.

Leave a legacy for your family and friends.
