Oral Histories for Family Legacies


An oral history preserve your life's story through recorded interviews.

A story that can be left to your family and many generations to come.


At the core of my approach is respect.

The client and I have an initial converstion about their life. I research their public profile, read their publications and research the period they were most active in. Together, we look at photos albums, memorabilia and other items that hold value to them.

We spend between 2-10 sessions, of varying length, recording their story. The client receives a soft and hard copy of their story.

Leave an oral history legacy.
