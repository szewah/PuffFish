At best it provides clarity, becoming the "connective tissue" that binds the organization together, and minimizes discrepencies in an organization's reputation. The effects may be non-inmediate, are indirect and different from person to person. Over the long term however, commmunication shapes thinking.

This approach is inspired by rapid prototyping, a method used to accelerate the innovation process in design and manufacturing.

From insights, and along with the clients intuition and experiences, we continue iterating, and build out the product and processes with the long term in mind.
